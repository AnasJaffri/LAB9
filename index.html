For All American Muscle Fans Dodge Charger THE DODGE CHARGER - BEST AMERICAN MUSCLE CAR

The Dodge Charger is probally one of the most iconic American muscle car in history. Introduced in the market in 1966 but production was hautled in the year 1987. When Dodge announced that it was bringing back the Charger name on a 2006 model-year four-door, there were enthusiasts out there who acted as if this were an unforgivable betrayal of the glorious Mopar heritage. That time it was introduced in 3 engine forms and trims. The First being the basic SE model which consisted of a 3.5L V6. The middle trim knwon as the the R/T(road and track) with a HEMI 5.7L V8 Then last but not least the beast of a machine the SRT which had a 6.2L V8 engine. Ever since Dodge has redeemed itself as big player in the American Muscle car industry. With cars like the Ford Mustang have been a very big competitor to it and is always compared to it.
